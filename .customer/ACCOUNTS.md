# Account Management

## Standard Roles

- **Customer Admin:** Manages users, permissions, and site-level settings.
- **Operator:** Executes approved workflows and monitors live operations.
- **Auditor:** Reviews logs, reports, and policy decisions.

## Access Lifecycle

1. Customer Admin submits onboarding request with business justification.
2. User receives setup instructions and completes authentication enrollment.
3. Role assignment is validated against least-privilege policy.
4. Access is reviewed at regular governance intervals.
5. Access is removed immediately on role change or offboarding.

## Operational Controls

- Keep role assignment evidence (request + approval record) for audits.
- Use named accounts; avoid shared credentials.
- Review dormant accounts and disable unused access.
- Limit elevated access to approved change windows.

## Escalation Data

When requesting access support, include:

- User identifier
- Site ID and tenant ID
- Requested role
- Reason for access change
- Time sensitivity and expected duration
