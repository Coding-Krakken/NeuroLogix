# Billing Overview

## Billing Model

Billing terms, schedules, and invoice structure are defined by your executed commercial agreement.

## Monthly Review Inputs

- Active sites and enabled modules
- Agreed support tier and response objectives
- Optional professional services or onboarding work
- Contracted add-ons enabled during the billing period

## Invoice Readiness Checklist

- Validate active site count and module status before invoice review.
- Confirm support tier alignment with current operational needs.
- Reconcile approved project services against statement line items.

## Billing Support

For billing inquiries, contact your account representative using your contracted support channel and include:

- Customer/account name
- Invoice period and identifier
- Question category (usage, support tier, services, adjustment)
- Relevant approval or change reference
